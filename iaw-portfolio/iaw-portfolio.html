Automated SMMA Client Reporting System

n8n Airtable Google Ads Meta Ads Slack Gemini AI

The Problem: The Manual Reporting Trap

Social Media Marketing Agencies (SMMAs) spend countless manual hours aggregating performance data from disparate ad platforms (Meta, Google, TikTok) and analytics tools. This process is repetitive, prone to human error, and eats into billable hours, leading to delayed and inconsistent client reports.

The Solution: An End-to-End Reporting Engine

An end-to-end reporting engine that runs on a schedule. It automatically connects to multiple advertising APIs to fetch key metrics, consolidates the data into a central database, and then generates and distributes comprehensive client reports without any human intervention.

Key Features

  • Automated data extraction from Meta Ads, Google Ads, TikTok Ads, and Google Analytics APIs.
  • Centralization of all client data into a single source of truth (Airtable or Google Sheets).
  • AI-powered summarization of weekly performance insights using a Large Language Model (e.g., Gemini).
  • Automated distribution of reports to clients via email and Slack, complete with key metrics and AI-generated summaries.
  • Connection to a live dashboard (Looker Studio) for real-time client access.

The Result: From Cost Center to Value Driver

Transforms the reporting process from a manual cost center into an efficient, automated system. It saves dozens of hours per month, eliminates data entry errors, and enhances client trust through timely, professional, and data-driven communication.

Influencer Agency Payment & Payout Engine

n8n Airtable QuickBooks Wise Twilio SMTP

The Problem: The Cash Flow Crunch

Influencer marketing agencies are often caught in a cash flow crunch between delayed brand payments and the need to pay influencers on time. Managing invoices, chasing brands, and tracking payouts manually via spreadsheets is inefficient and damages relationships with both clients and creators.

The Solution: A Complete Financial Workflow Automation

A complete financial workflow automation that manages the entire lifecycle of a brand deal, from invoicing the brand to paying the influencer. The system monitors payment statuses and uses conditional logic to trigger reminders, escalations, and payouts automatically.

Key Features

  • Automatic invoice creation in QuickBooks upon deal approval in a CRM (Airtable).
  • Scheduled, multi-stage payment reminders to brands via email for upcoming and overdue invoices.
  • Automated WhatsApp escalation messages for severely overdue payments.
  • Real-time monitoring of invoice payment status via the QuickBooks API.
  • Automatic triggering of influencer payouts via the Wise API as soon as a brand's payment is confirmed.
  • Automated payout confirmation emails to influencers, providing transparency and reducing inquiries.

The Result: Building Trust Through Automation

Solves a critical cash flow and relationship management problem. It drastically reduces the accounts receivable cycle, ensures influencers are paid promptly, minimizes administrative workload, and builds a foundation of trust and professionalism.

Event Management Vendor Coordination Hub

n8n Airtable Slack Gemini AI SMTP

The Problem: The Chaos of Coordination

Event management involves coordinating dozens of vendors with unique deadlines, deliverables, and payment schedules. Tracking this via spreadsheets and WhatsApp is chaotic, leading to missed deadlines, on-site confusion, and payment errors that can jeopardize an entire event.

The Solution: A Centralized Operating System

A centralized operating system for event vendor management. When a new event is created, the workflow automatically generates and assigns all necessary vendor tasks from templates, monitors deadlines, manages the payment approval process, and generates post-event reports.

Key Features

  • Templated task generation in Airtable for all vendor categories when a new event is created.
  • Automated deadline reminders sent directly to vendors via email.
  • Internal escalation alerts to event managers via Slack for overdue tasks.
  • Streamlined payment approval workflow that notifies the finance team when deliverables are submitted.
  • Capability to power a live, mobile-friendly, on-site check-in dashboard.
  • Automated post-event vendor performance and budget reporting.

The Result: Flawless Events, Scalable Operations

Replaces operational chaos with a scalable, reliable system. It minimizes the risk of missed deadlines, improves vendor relationships through clear communication and timely payments, and provides management with data-driven insights for future events.

Influencer Brand Deal & Content Lifecycle OS

n8n Airtable Google Calendar Gemini AI SMTP

The Problem: Juggling Deals and Deadlines

Individual influencers struggle to manage multiple brand deals across various platforms. Tracking deliverables, approval cycles, posting schedules, and payment terms in notes or spreadsheets often leads to missed deadlines, incorrect content, and damaged brand relationships.

The Solution: A Personal Business-in-a-Box

A personal business-in-a-box for influencers. This workflow automates the entire content lifecycle, from syncing a new deal to the calendar, managing the content approval process, reminding the brand to pay, and finally, reporting on the post's performance.

Key Features

  • Centralized brand deal tracking in Airtable.
  • Automatic syncing of draft and posting deadlines to Google Calendar.
  • Automated reminders to the influencer for upcoming content deadlines.
  • AI-powered compliance check on draft captions to ensure all required hashtags and mentions are included before sending to the brand.
  • Automated email workflow to manage the brand's draft approval process.
  • Automated payment reminders for overdue invoices.
  • Automated fetching of post-performance analytics and generation of a branded report for the client.

The Result: Operate Like a Professional Business

Empowers influencers to operate like a professional business. It eliminates administrative overhead, prevents costly mistakes, streamlines brand communication, and adds a layer of professionalism that strengthens relationships and encourages repeat business.
